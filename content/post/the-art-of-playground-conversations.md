This is a page about »The Art of Playground Conversations«.

When you talk on the playground, your conversation is inevitably interrupted by a kid.

So you need to get really good at distilling your conversation into bite-sized chunks that will still be coherent if you get interrupted.

Suspenseful stories?  Nope.

Complex explanations about things?  Again, nope.
